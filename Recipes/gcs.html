Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 4 slices White bread
  • 3 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 2 slices Cheddar cheese
  1. Preheat a nonstick skillet over medium heat.
    Generously butter one side of a slice of bread.
    Place bread butter-side down in the hot skillet; add 1 slice of cheese. Butter a second slice of bread on one side and place butter-side up on top of cheese.

  2. Cook until lightly browned on one side; flip over and continue cooking until cheese is melted.
    Repeat with remaining 2 slices of bread, butter, and slice of cheese.
